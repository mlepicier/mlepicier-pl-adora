General Questions

What is an AI Playbook?

An AI Playbook is a curated collection of Custom GPT Assistants designed for a specific area of life (Finance, Productivity, Fitness, etc.). Each Playbook contains 3-5 expert-built GPTs, organized documentation, and step-by-step guides to help you leverage AI effectively in that domain.

Do I need ChatGPT Plus?

Most GPTs in our Playbooks work with the free version of ChatGPT. However, some advanced features and capabilities may require ChatGPT Plus for optimal performance. We clearly indicate which GPTs benefit most from Plus in each Playbook.

How do I access the Playbooks?

After purchase, you'll instantly receive a download link via email. Your Playbook contains direct links to all Custom GPT Assistants, along with documentation and guides. Simply click the links to open the GPTs in ChatGPT and start using them immediately.

Are updates included?

Yes! Early buyers get lifetime updates at no extra cost. We continuously improve our GPTs, fix issues, and add new assistants to your Playbooks. You'll be notified via email when updates are available.

Technical Questions

Do I need technical skills to use the GPTs?

No! Our GPTs are designed to be user-friendly and require no technical knowledge. If you can chat in ChatGPT, you can use our Playbooks. Each Playbook includes clear instructions and examples to get you started.

Can I use the GPTs on mobile?

Yes! Custom GPTs work on any device where you can access ChatGPT—desktop, mobile browser, or the ChatGPT mobile app.
